Hello and welcome to 'The Miracle' a website named after the beautiful art garden 'The Miracle Garden' in Erasmus park!

About

This site is dedicated to giving light to the art piece that is 'The Miracle Garden'. This garden is a beautiful art piece by the artist 'Elspeth Diederix' located in Amsterdam-West.
